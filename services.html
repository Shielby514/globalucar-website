Kingmay Auto Parts — OEM-Grade Wholesale

Specialist wholesale of OEM-grade parts for Japanese & Korean vehicles

Kingmay is the dedicated auto parts arm of Globalucar Group. We source OEM-grade engine, cooling, suspension and electrical components from verified Chinese manufacturers whose production lines serve major vehicle brands. All parts are manufactured to match original equipment specifications.

  • 60+ SKUs: engine, cooling, suspension & electrical systems
  • OEM-grade quality: dimensional checks, material certifications (SGS/TÜV)
  • Full OEM cross-reference support & compatible vehicle matrix
  • MOQ from 50 pcs — flexible for importers and distributors
  • Private label & custom packaging available on bulk orders
Sourcing transparency: Kingmay sources from qualified Chinese manufacturers, not directly from Korean OEM factories. Our key supplier holds authorised supply agreements with select Korean OEMs — same production lines, full traceability.

Quality Control & Export Packing

Practical, field-tested processes — not over-promises

Inspection & Traceability

  • ✓ Incoming material verification with supplier batch tracking
  • ✓ 100% dimensional inspection on critical components
  • ✓ Functional testing for safety-related parts (braking, suspension)
  • ✓ Full batch traceability — every part traceable to production date and material lot
  • ✓ Factory visit and video inspection available by appointment

Export Packaging & Logistics

  • ✓ Individual product wrapping with shock-absorption protection
  • ✓ ISPM-15 compliant export packaging (heat-treated timber for APEC markets)
