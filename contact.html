Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

For cars, the MOQ is 1 unit. For auto parts, MOQ varies by product (typically 100–00 pcs). Contact us for specific MOQ details.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept T/T (Bank Transfer) and L/C (Letter of Credit) for orders above USD 50,000. A 30% deposit is typically required to confirm an order.

Do you handle all the export documentation?

Yes –we prepare commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin, export customs declaration and any additional documents required by your country.

What is the typical lead time?

New cars: 4– weeks from order confirmation. Used cars: 2– weeks. Auto parts: 2– weeks depending on volume and sea/air freight selection.

Do you offer inspection before shipping?

Yes. We provide a full pre-delivery inspection (PDI) report with photos. Third-party inspection (e.g. SGS, BV) can be arranged at buyer's request.

Can you ship to any country?

We ship to 50+ countries worldwide. We can advise on import restrictions and regulations for your target destination.
