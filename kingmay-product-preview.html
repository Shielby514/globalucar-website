About This Part

The KingMay 49-1001 is a premium aftermarket engine oil cooler designed as a direct replacement for VW and Audi vehicles. Built from high-grade aluminum alloy, it delivers efficient heat dissipation to maintain optimal engine oil temperature under all operating conditions.

  • Direct bolt-on replacement — no modifications required
  • Manufactured to OEM dimensional specifications
  • Rigorous quality testing including pressure and thermal cycling
  • Compatible with both gasoline and diesel engine applications
  • Suitable for passenger cars and light commercial vehicles
  • Neutral packaging with optional custom branding for bulk orders
