About Our Cooling System Parts Catalog

Cooling system failures account for a significant share of roadside breakdowns globally. Importers and workshop chains in tropical and high-mileage markets — Southeast Asia, Middle East, Latin America and Sub-Saharan Africa — consume cooling parts at high volumes year-round.

Our cooling system catalog covers: heat exchangers (aluminum radiators, intercoolers, oil coolers), coolant circulation (water pump assemblies with housing, thermostat & housing kits), airflow (electric & mechanical cooling fans, fan clutches) and plumbing (upper/lower hoses, overflow tanks, radiator caps). All parts carry OEM cross-references and ship with material certificates on request.
