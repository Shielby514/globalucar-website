About Our Electrical System Parts Catalog

Modern vehicles contain hundreds of electrical and electronic components, and sensor-related fault codes are now the most common reason for workshop visits worldwide. As emerging markets rapidly increase vehicle parc age, demand for electrical replacement parts — particularly sensors, alternators and lighting — is growing faster than any other aftermarket segment.

Our electrical catalog covers: generation & starting (alternators, starter motors, voltage regulators), sensors & management (oxygen/lambda sensors, MAF sensors, crankshaft/camshaft position sensors, ABS speed sensors, throttle position sensors), chassis electrical (power window motors, wiper motors, blower motors) and lighting (headlight assemblies, tail light assemblies, turn signals, reverse lamps). All sensor SKUs include OEM part number cross-references.
