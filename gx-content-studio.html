🔍 SEO Publishing Guide for Blog Posts

✅ Checklist for Each New Article

  • Meta Title: 50–60 characters. Include primary keyword + brand. E.g.: "BYD EV Parts Sourcing Guide 2026 | Globalucar"
  • Meta Description: 145–160 characters. Natural sentence with 2 keywords. No keyword stuffing.
  • URL Slug: Lowercase, hyphenated, no stop words. E.g.: byd-ev-parts-sourcing-2026
  • Content Length: Minimum 800 words for SEO. 1500+ words for competitive keywords.
  • H2 Headings: Use 3–5 H2 sections. Include secondary keywords naturally.
  • Internal Links: Link to relevant product pages (e.g. products.html?cat=parts)
  • Images: Use descriptive alt text with keywords.
  • Publish Frequency: 1–2 articles per week for consistent SEO gains.
