About Us

Guangzhou Vehicle External Trade Co., Ltd

Who We Are

China's One-Stop Vehicle & Auto Parts Export Group

Founded in Guangzhou, Guangzhou Vehicle External Trade Co., Ltd —known globally as Globalucar— is a licensed exporter operating two complementary business arms: Globalucar Vehicle (whole-vehicle export) and Kingmay Auto Parts (specialist parts wholesale).

Operating from Nansha Port, the largest RoRo terminal in South China, we combine port proximity, in-house logistics expertise and overseas warehouse infrastructure to deliver fast, reliable export service to buyers worldwide.

Over the past decade we have shipped to 50+ countries across Africa, the Middle East, Southeast Asia, Eastern Europe and Latin America, building long-term partnerships with importers, dealers and fleet operators.
