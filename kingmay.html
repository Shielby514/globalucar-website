Kingmay Quality Standards

We don't make "Korea OEM" claims we can't back up — we prove quality through certifications, specs, and traceability.

📋

OEM-Grade Specifications

All parts are manufactured to match or exceed original equipment specifications for fit, form and function — with full technical datasheets on request.

🔬

Material & Dimensional QC

100% dimensional checks, material certifications (SGS/TÜV available) and batch traceability ensure consistent quality across every shipment.

🏭

Verified Supplier Network

Our key suppliers include factories that hold direct OEM supply agreements with major vehicle brands — the same production lines, different packaging.

📦

Export Packing & Labelling

Parts are packed to ISPM-15 compliant export standards, clearly labelled with part numbers and compatible vehicle references for hassle-free customs clearance.

🌐

Global Export Compliance

CE, ECE and DOT certifications available on selected lines. We handle CO, packing lists and commercial invoices for smooth import clearance worldwide.

💬

Dedicated B2B Support

Dedicated account managers for wholesale buyers. Technical queries, cross-reference support and OEM number look-ups handled within 24 hours.

⚠️ Transparent Sourcing Statement: Kingmay sources from qualified Chinese manufacturers, not directly from Korean OEM factories. Our major supplier holds authorised supply agreements with select Korean OEMs — but Kingmay itself is not a direct Korean OEM licensee. We prioritise transparency: you will always know exactly what you are buying.

Why Global Buyers Trust Kingmay

Over a decade of实战 experience in the automotive aftermarket — built on factory-side control, flexible sourcing and reliable export execution.

🏭

Factory-Integrated Sourcing

We are not just resellers. Kingmay coordinates manufacturing resources, supply chain partners and quality control under one roof, giving you direct access to production-side pricing and delivery timelines without the overhead of managing multiple suppliers yourself.

🚗

Deep Korean & Japanese Focus

Our primary strength lies in Hyundai-Kia full-line chassis and drivetrain components, extended to Toyota, Nissan and Honda applications. This focused expertise means faster fitment verification, better stock availability and more competitive pricing than generalist suppliers.

🔍

Proactive Parts Matching Service

Unsure which part fits your vehicle? Send us your VIN, chassis number, or even a photo of the old part — our sourcing team will identify the correct OEM reference and compatible alternatives within 24 hours. We do the verification work so you avoid costly ordering mistakes and return hassles.

📦

Consolidated Shipment Capability

Ordering across multiple product categories? We can combine engine parts, suspension components, cooling and electrical items into a single consolidated shipment from Guangzhou. One container, one set of customs documents, lower per-unit logistics cost.

🛡️

Quality Assurance with Pre-Shipment Verification

Every order undergoes pre-shipment inspection with dimensional checks and functional testing. We provide material certificates (SGS/TÜV available on request), batch traceability records, and detailed inspection reports — so you know exactly what is in the container before it leaves Guangzhou. First-time buyers can request sample validation before committing to bulk orders.

💬

Responsive B2B Support
